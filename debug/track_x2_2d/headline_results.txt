KEY FINDINGS
------------
1. Cusp correction is solver-independent: identical dE_cusp at same l_max
   (difference = 0.000000 mHa at l_max=2, R=1.4)

2. Correction magnitude scales as ~1/(l_max+1/2)^4:
   - l_max=2: -1.74 mHa at R_eq
   - l_max=4: -0.39 mHa at R_eq
   - Ratio: 0.223

3. Paper 15 projection of ~95.1% was INCORRECT. It applied an l_max=2
   correction differential (1.74 mHa) to the l_max=4 baseline. The
   correct l_max=4 correction differential is only 0.39 mHa, giving 94.3%.

4. The validated best D_e for H2 in the GeoVac framework is:
   94.3% with 2D variational solver + Schwartz cusp correction at l_max=4
